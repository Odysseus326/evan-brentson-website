About Me

“I am a Software Developer and a musician. I have other skills and hobbies too, but these two define who I am best.”

My Beginnings

In 6th grade, I joined my middle school band's percussion section. I played through 8th grade where I would graduate from middle school. I also took a 'Gateway to Technology’ class during this time that would spark my interest in tech.


When I entered high school, I joined the band's percussion program, where I would remain until graduation. I learned how to play several percussion instruments and eventually became leader of my section. A lot my academic focus was spent dabbling in trades and tech. Construction, mechanics, sciences. It wasn't until a programming course sparked my interest for development


I graduated high school and left for university with the intention of furthering my music skills and getting a degree in computer science.

My Journey

I attended Washington State University for my freshman year of college, studying in pre-requisite courses for a computer science degree and playing as a snare drummer for the Cougar Marching Band.


I left WSU after the first year for a more affordable option. I got a job in retail and enrolled at Pierce College in Puyallup. I continued my studies in computer science, and eventually added music as a major. I became heavily involved in the music department and the wider music community, performing with many groups. Eventually I would graduate with my AS in Science and AA in Music.


I held my job in retail for nearly six years. Starting as a cashier, I eventually became the department lead for the deli department.

Music or Engineering... Why not both!

My Present

Following my Graduation from Pierce College, I resigned from my job as a Deli Department lead to pursue my career in Software Development.
